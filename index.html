INTRODUCTION

What are engineering drawings?
  • A type of technical drawing
  • A graphical language, not just pictures
  • Standardizd, detailed, and unmistakable
  • Convey the following common information:
    • Geoemtry
    • Dimensions
    • Tolerances
    • Material
    • Finish

Why do drawings matter?
  • Communicate the dimensions and features of a part or assembly
  • Standardized, communicable
  • Allow parts to be manufactured by third-party
  • Part of design process
  • Design for Manufacturing (DFM)
  • Record keeping & version tracking
